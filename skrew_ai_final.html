Meet the Skrew

Meet the Skrew 🤙

The Skrew.ai "krew" is a group of friends, parents, and tech veterans who came together around a shared mission: protecting human authenticity in the age of AI.

Between us, we bring decades of experience in online video, video advertising, and internet technologies. But more importantly, we're parents who worry about our children's digital identities, friends who've seen misinformation spread unchecked, and technologists who understand both the promise and peril of AI.

When deepfakes started becoming indistinguishable from reality, we knew we had to act. We believe AI can be a force for good, but we also recognize the urgent need to defend against its misuse. That's why we built SkrewAI Camera – a free tool that ensures real human moments stay verifiably human.

Our technology is free to download and free to use. We believe everyone deserves access to truth verification – from journalists documenting events in conflict zones to parents sharing family moments online. No subscriptions, no paywalls. Just honest technology for an increasingly dishonest digital world.
